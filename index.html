Past Projects

Code related things I've done in the past

My greatest project to date is an Excel based point of sale (POS) system the dispensary I helped manage was able to operate off of for two months when the United States' largest cannabis POS system was hacked in January 2017.

It began when I showed up for the daily morning inventory count and noticed the website wasn't working. Fortunately I had a great team player on the morning manager shift with me. He gave space to create a paper form we could use to track sales. Once that was in place I made it electronic.

Two weeks later we were live on my system. Over the course of the next month I fleshed out the details. From that point the focus became taking data from a few thousand excel files, and generating reports for the company's accountant, the Nevada's tax department and marijuana compliance division (now the Cannabis Compliance Board).
